# Gold Purity Guide (UK Hallmarks Explained)

When selling gold, understanding purity is key to knowing its true value.

## What is Gold Purity?

Gold purity is measured in carats (ct):

- 24ct = 100% gold  
- 18ct = 75% gold  
- 14ct = 58.5% gold  
- 9ct = 37.5% gold  

## UK Hallmarks

Most gold items in the UK are stamped with a hallmark indicating purity.

Common stamps include:

- 375 (9ct)
- 585 (14ct)
- 750 (18ct)
- 916 (22ct)

## Why Purity Matters

Higher purity gold has more intrinsic value, meaning:

- Heavier payout  
- Better resale value
